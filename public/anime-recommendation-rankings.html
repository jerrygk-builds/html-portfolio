My Recommended Anime Picks According to Jerry Kibuchi

If you haven't watched anime before - it's in no particular order

1. Bleach

It an action packed adventure of Kurosaki Ichigo and his friends - Kuchiki Rukia, Kon, Ishida Uryuu, Inuoe Orihime, Sado Yasutora on their own solo and joint journeys as they self-discover, self-realize and awaken their unique powers which they use to protect the living world and sometimes the spirit world from malevolent forces.

2. Demon Slayer (Kimetsu no Yaiba)

Follow the journey of Tanjiro Kamado, a good-natured kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer to avenge his slain family slaughtered by demons, and his little sister, Nezuko, was left transformed into a demon. Along his journey to becoming a demon slayer, he is accompanied by comrades-turned-friends Zenitsu Agatsuma and Inosuke Hashibira on their own journeys to be demon slayers.

3. The World's Finest Assassin Gets Reincarnated in Another World as an Aristocrat (Sekai Saikou no Ansatsusha, Isekai Kizoku ni Tensei suru)

A skilled assassin is reincarnated in a fantasy world with the mission to prevent the destruction of that world by eliminating its hero. Reincarnated into the Tuatha Dé nobile family as Lugh Tuatha Dé, he uses his knowledge and skills from his past life to live free, choosing his path while trying to fulfill his mission. Will he succeed in his mission?

4. Solo Leveling

In a world where hunters with magical abilities fight deadly monsters, Sung Jin-Woo, given the title - "The Weakest Hunter of All Mankind" by fellow hunters, embarks on a journey to become the strongest after a normal dungeon-clearing raid ended up in a tragic traumatic event that granted him unique powers.

5. How a Realist Hero Rebuilt the Kingdom (Genjitsu Shugi Yuusha no Oukoku Saikenki)

This follows the story of how an 18-year-old Kazuya Souma, an aspiring civil servant, gets teleported (isekai'd) to a strange medieval world filled will swords, magic, monsters and demons. Currently, there is the pressing threat of demonic invasion from the northern third of the supercontinent of Landia called the Demon Lord's Domain. Kazuya has his hands full trying to stabilize the economy, deal with food insecurity and threats of invasion from neighbouring countries. As the newly appointed king of the nation of Elfrieden, what will he do?

6. That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime (Tensei shitara Slime Datta Ken)

A man in his mid-thirties is stabbed to death and reincarnated in a fantasy world as a powerful slime monster named Rimuru Tempest. Using his unique abilities, Rimuru sets out to create a new nation where all races can coexist peacefully.

7. Tokyo Revengers

Takemichi Hanagaki, a 26-year-old freeter, learns that his middle school ex-girlfriend Hinata Tachibana and her younger brother Naoto have been killed by the Tokyo Manji Gang. After a near-death experience, Takemichi finds himself transported back in time to his middle school days. Determined to change the future and save Hinata, he sets out to infiltrate the Tokyo Manji Gang and alter the course of events.

8. Blue Lock

After Japan's crushing defeat in the 2018 World Cup, the Japanese Football Union initiates a radical new training program called "Blue Lock" to create the world's best striker. The program brings together 300 of Japan's most talented high school forwards and pits them against each other in a high-stakes competition where only one will emerge as the ultimate striker.

9. Tomodachi Game

Yuuichi Katagiri, a high school student who values friendship above all else, finds himself entangled in a mysterious game that tests the bonds of friendship and trust. Along with his friends, Yuuichi is forced to participate in a series of psychological challenges where they must confront their deepest fears and secrets in order to win the game and escape with their lives.

10. Kaiji: Ulitimate Survivor

Kaiji Itou, a down-and-out man burdened by debt, is given a chance to turn his life around when he is invited to participate in a high-stakes gambling event on a mysterious ship. As Kaiji navigates the dangerous world of underground gambling, he must use his wits and cunning to outsmart his opponents and win big, all while facing life-threatening challenges that test his resolve and determination.
